Hello, I'm Tal

Assistant Professor, Radboud University • Visiting Professor, Yale

I'm a curious mind exploring the intersection of intelligence and complexity. Based in Nijmegen, I spend my days researching multiagent systems, reinforcement learning, and complex systems—bridging the worlds of artificial intelligence and theoretical foundations.

My research focuses on how intelligent systems learn, adapt, and interact in complex environments. From classical reinforcement learning to quantum machine learning, I'm fascinated by the mathematical structures that govern learning and decision-making.

When I'm not lost in research, you'll find me plotting strategic moves on a chessboard or exploring new places around the world. Feel free to connect with me through various platforms.
